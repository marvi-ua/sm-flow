Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Flowcast ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our automated AI-powered entertainment content platform.

1. Information We Collect

Flowcast is an automated content publishing platform. We collect minimal personal data:

  • Platform account data: When our application connects to social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram) via their official APIs, we access only the permissions required to publish content — specifically, the ability to upload and post video content to the authorized account.
  • Performance analytics: We access publicly available post performance metrics (views, engagement) from connected platform accounts for the purpose of content optimization.
  • No end-user data: Flowcast does not collect personal data from viewers or followers of published content.

2. How We Use Information

Data accessed through platform APIs is used exclusively to:

  • Publish original AI-generated entertainment video content to authorized social media accounts.
  • Retrieve performance metrics to improve content quality and publishing schedules.
  • Ensure compliance with platform posting policies and rate limits.

We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. TikTok API Data Usage

Flowcast uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content to authorized TikTok accounts. Data accessed via this API is limited to what is strictly necessary for content publishing. We comply with TikTok's Developer Terms of Service and Platform Policy. TikTok API data is not used for advertising, user profiling, or any purpose beyond automated content publishing and performance tracking.

4. Data Storage and Security

API credentials and access tokens are stored securely using environment variable secrets management on private, access-controlled infrastructure. We do not store platform user data beyond what is required for operational logging.

5. Data Retention

Operational logs (post status, timestamps, performance metrics) are retained for up to 90 days for debugging and optimization purposes, then permanently deleted.

6. Third-Party Services

Flowcast integrates with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • TikTok — tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Meta (Instagram) — facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Ayrshare — ayrshare.com/privacy

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect any social media account from Flowcast at any time by revoking API permissions directly in the relevant platform's app settings. This immediately and permanently terminates our access to that account.

8. Children's Privacy

Flowcast does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our platform is intended for use by adults and content creators only.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests may be directed to: apple_inc@ukr.net
