How it works

From trending topic to published video

Flowcast runs a daily pipeline of specialized AI agents, each owning one part of the content lifecycle.

1

Trend Detection

Scans trending topics daily across platforms and scores them for virality and audience fit.

2

Script Generation

AI writes a complete video script — hook, body, and call to action — optimized for short-form retention.

3

Voice & Music

Text-to-speech voiceover is generated and paired with mood-matched background music from licensed sources.

4

Video Editing

Stock footage, text overlays, and audio are assembled into a finished 1080×1920 vertical video.

5

Quality Review

An AI quality gate checks every video before it is posted — hook strength, audio balance, caption readability.

6

Auto-Publishing

Approved videos are scheduled and posted to TikTok and Instagram at optimal times via official platform APIs.

Features

Built for creators who want to scale

Every component is designed to run without daily intervention, with human override available at any stage.

🤖

Fully AI-generated content

Scripts, voiceover, visuals, and music are all produced by AI — no broadcast footage, no copyright risk, no manual editing.

📅

Smart scheduling

Posts are timed to audience activity windows and randomized to avoid automation detection patterns.

🎯

Niche-configurable

The entire pipeline is driven by a topic configuration — swap the niche and the whole system adapts, no code changes needed.

📊

Analytics feedback loop

Performance data from TikTok and Instagram flows back into the trend scoring model, improving future content automatically.

🔒

API-compliant posting

All publishing happens through official platform APIs. No browser automation, no Terms of Service violations.

⚡

Error handling & alerts

Each agent retries failed jobs with exponential backoff. Failures are logged and flagged via Telegram notifications.

About Flowcast

One pipeline.
Multiple channels.

Flowcast is an automated content publishing platform built to create and distribute original AI-generated entertainment video content at scale. Our system uses a chain of AI agents — each responsible for a discrete step — to go from daily trend discovery to a published video without human involvement.

All content produced by Flowcast is original. We use commercially licensed stock footage, AI-generated voiceover, and licensed music. We do not source, redistribute, or repurpose any broadcast media.

Flowcast integrates with TikTok and Instagram via their official developer APIs, posting only to accounts that have explicitly authorized access. We comply fully with each platform's developer policies and terms of service.
